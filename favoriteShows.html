Favorite Stories

My favorite kind of TV show is anime. Anime consists of many different genres, and has been popular since the 1950s!

  • "Astro Boy" was of the first manga/anime series, serialized in 1952. It was written by Osamu Tezuka! One of my favorite artists, Yoh Yoshinari was a partner of Tezuka.
  • I started watching anime when the famous anime, "Attack on Titan" was in the process of releasing their second season (Around 2017). I watched AOT, Free, Ouran Highschool Host Club, and some of Kiss Him Not Me before taking a break to revisit anime during the quarantine.
  • I'm also an avid manga reader. My favorite mangas include "Blue Period", "One Punch Man" and "Wotakoi". Blue Period was recently released as an anime, and Wotakoi has one season in anime. I prefer the manga version of both though.
